About John Silverton

John Silverton is the author of the Dr. Jack Lambert geopolitical thrillers, blending espionage, medicine, and international conflict.

Born and raised in the UK, John moved to the United States in 1976 and now lives in California. A retired plastic surgeon, he spends his time writing, painting in oils, and rowing, while enjoying travel with his wife and time with his family and Border Collies.

His novels explore the hidden worlds of intelligence, power, and consequence—where information can be more dangerous than bullets.
